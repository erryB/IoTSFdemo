	-- SETCONFIG

.\IoTDemoConsole.exe setconfig -serviceUri=fabric:/EBIoTApplication/DeviceActor -deviceId=Batman -tempThreshold=30 -humThreshold=70  

set batman configuration with temperature threshold equals to 30 degree and humidity threshold equals to 70 %.

.\IoTDemoConsole.exe getconfig -serviceUri=fabric:/EBIoTApplication/DeviceActor -deviceId=Batman 

retrieve the configuration of Batman Device and shows on the screen
